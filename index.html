The Platform

What CourtIQ Does

Upload footage from any source. CourtIQ's AI goes to work instantly — tagging, analyzing, and surfacing what matters before your next game.

Auto Possession Tagging

Every possession, set play, transition, and defensive scheme is automatically tagged the moment footage is uploaded. No manual work required.

Natural Language Search

Ask your film library anything. "Show me all pick-and-roll coverages where we gave up corner 3s" — CourtIQ surfaces the exact clips in seconds.

Auto Scouting Reports

Pre-game prep packets generated automatically. Opponent tendencies, player habits, and defensive breakdowns — ready before you ever open a laptop.

Real-Time Shot Scoring

Every shot is scored for quality — not just made or missed. Understand shot creation, shot selection, and defensive breakdowns at a granular level.

Defensive Heatmaps

Auto-generated heatmaps per opponent reveal exactly where your defense gives up points — and where theirs does too.

In-Game Alerts

Adjustment alerts pushed to coaching staff in real time. What's working, what's not — surfaced automatically while the game is still in front of you.
